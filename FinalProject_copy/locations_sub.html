I with my sister and parents lived in a small town,Luo Jiang. It is a quiet but pretty place in Sichuan Province, China. The bridge and small mountains as well as the blocks of green crops made my childhood memory full of colours.

After graduating from university, I worked and lived in Chengdu, the captial of Sichuan, the most-populated province in China. Meantime, my sister started work and had her own family at Deyang, a middle sized city, locating between Luo Jiang and Cheng Du city, Sichuan province. After my parents retired, they moved to Deyang (the right star shown in the following world map) and live in a same city with my sister.

While my sister and her family can visit my parents with a walking distance, I separate with them with 8 thousands of kilomiles between. Vancouver Vancouver, Canada, the left star of in the following world map, is the place I currently with my family live.
