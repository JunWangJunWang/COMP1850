Deyang locates northeast Chengdu, captial of Sichuan province. It is famous for a few high-tech heavy machinery companies there. It has around 4 million people and area of 2 thousands square miles.

I grew up in a small town in Sichuan. After the university, while I worked at Chengud, my sister started her career and family at Deyang, a county near to Chengdutives live. Since that, I visited there very frequently, and soon it became more and more familiar to me especailly after my parents moved there after both of them retired.

Deyang is also well known because of a very good alcohol brand Jiananchun. The Sanxingdui museum, ancient bronze mask, attracts thousands of tourists every year.
