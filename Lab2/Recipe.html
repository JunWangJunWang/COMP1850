How to Make Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups mashed bananas
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 cup macadamia nuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ยบ.
  2. Grease and flour a loaf pan.
  3. In a bowl, mix butter and sugar until creamy.
  4. In a separate bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  5. Blend dry ingredients with creamed butter/sugar mixture.
  6. Add mashed bananas, eggs and nuts.
  7. Pour into loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour or until a knife comes out clean.
