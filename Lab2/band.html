Stream of Praise

Stream of Praise (SOP) is one of my favourite groups. They are a band but more like a music team for Christian ministry. They were established in 1993 as a non-profit organization in California. The main singers are:
  • Sandy Yu
  • Tiffany M.Cheng
  • David Yu
  • Grace Tseng
  • Sandy Yu

So far, they have published 680 songs and 51 releases. Here are the most recent 3 albums:

The Path of Grace
This is a instrumental album, including the 12 most popular songs in the previous published but this time in strings. You will experience those old touching songs in a different way.
Glory
From Now till Evermore is the most powerful song in this 14-song album. This is one of the releases in 2014. It belong to Praise and Worship Genres. From the quick speeded Glory to slow Emmanuel, each song tell a different story, either cheering you up or leading you a quite calm status. It is a new but pretty good release.
