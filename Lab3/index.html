I graduated from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China UESTC. Over there I have not only had my academic degrees but also I became an university teacher and researcher as my first career.

It locates in Chengdu, the provincial capital of Sichuan, China.

It was founded in 1956 with cooperation of Electronic division from three universities in China. It was one of the seven earliest main universities in national defense and now it is identified as one of the top universities in China with special support to be world-class academics and research-oriented comprehensive university.It originally started from only electronic information engineering now to multidisciplinary university.
